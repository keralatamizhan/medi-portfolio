Research & Projects

01 🌾

Snacking Habits and Nutritional Status among College Students

An assessment of the snacking habits and nutritional status among college students was conducted to understand the dietary practices of young adults. The study involved data collection through surveys and food diaries to evaluate typical snacking patterns. Subsequently, the nutritional status of the students was assessed using anthropometric measurements and dietary analysis. The findings provided insights into the impact of snacking habits on the overall nutritional profile of college students.

AssessmentAnalysisStatistics
02 🩺

Assessment of Osteoarthritis in Overweight Adults and Nutritional Intervention

Assessed pain intensity and quality of life in 35 overweight adults with OA. Instituted a 12-week dietary intervention focusing on omega-3 fatty acids, antioxidants, and weight management. Evaluated changes in BMI, pain scores (WOMAC), and functional ability.
